## 🤖 Vision: The Future of AI & Digital Government

The question that drives my work: *Where is AI taking us, and how do we bring everyone along?*

I'm fascinated by the potential of artificial intelligence to solve genuine problems in the public sector. The key challenge isn't just building better technology—it's making digital transformation *fashionable* to both the curious and the cautious.

**For the Curious:** Those excited by innovation, eager to explore AI capabilities, and ready to push boundaries.

**For the Cautious:** Those rightfully concerned about change, risk management, governance, and ensuring solutions work reliably within existing systems.

The convergence of machine learning, cloud infrastructure, and human-centered design holds extraordinary promise—but only if we bring the whole organization along on the journey.
